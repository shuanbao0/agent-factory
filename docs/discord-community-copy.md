## English Version

**Agent Factory -- Run a 64-Agent AI Company from Your Terminal**

Ever wished you had an entire team working on your project? Agent Factory is an open-source, self-contained multi-agent platform that gives you 64 specialized AI agents -- CEO, engineers, designers, testers, marketers, even a quant trading team -- all collaborating autonomously.

What sets it apart:
- **Fully local**: No cloud dependencies. One command to start: `npx agent-factory start`
- **Autopilot mode**: Give the CEO a goal, watch 64 agents decompose, assign, and execute tasks in parallel
- **Pixel-art office UI**: See your AI org at a glance with a retro visualization
- **15+ LLM providers**: Anthropic, OpenAI, DeepSeek, MiniMax, and more
- **Real artifacts**: Agents produce actual PRDs, code, designs, and reports

Built with Next.js + TypeScript. Runs on macOS/Linux.

GitHub: https://github.com/shuanbao0/agent-factory

## Chinese Version (中文版)

**Agent Factory -- 用终端运行一家 64 人 AI 公司**

一个人也能拥有一整个团队！Agent Factory 是一个完全开源的多 Agent 协作平台，内置 64 个专业角色模板 -- CEO、工程师、设计师、测试、市场、法务、量化团队 -- 自主协作，并行执行。

核心亮点：
- **完全本地运行**：无需云服务，`npx agent-factory start` 一键启动
- **Autopilot 模式**：给 CEO 一句话目标，自动分解任务、分配角色、并行执行
- **像素办公室 UI**：复古像素风可视化，直观展示 Agent 工作状态
- **15+ 模型支持**：Anthropic、OpenAI、DeepSeek、MiniMax 等
- **真实产出**：PRD 文档、代码、设计稿、分析报告

Next.js + TypeScript 构建，支持 macOS/Linux。

GitHub: https://github.com/shuanbao0/agent-factory
